🏞️ About Lake Baikal 🏞️

Lake Baikal is the world's deepest and oldest lake, located in Siberia, Russia.

  • 🌊 It contains 20% of the world's unfrozen freshwater
  • 🐟 Home to over 2,500 species of animals
  • 🏔️ Surrounded by beautiful mountains
  • ❄️ Freezes in winter (Gennady loves ice fishing!)

🌍 Conservation Information 🌍

Baikal seals (Pusa sibirica) are the only freshwater seals in the world!
