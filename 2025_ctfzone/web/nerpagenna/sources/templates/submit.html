✉️ Submit a Letter to Gennady ✉️

Gennady loves receiving letters from his fans!

🐟 He can't read, but he appreciates the effort! 🐟

📝 Please submit your letter as a .docx file

Gennady only accepts .docx files! (He's very picky about file formats)

📋 Letter Guidelines

  • ✅ Write about how much you love seals
  • ✅ Tell Gennady about your day
  • ✅ Ask him about his favorite fish
  • ✅ Share your conservation efforts
  • ❌ Don't mention pollution (he gets sad)
  • ❌ Don't ask him to read your letter (he can't)

📝 Sample Letter

Dear Gennady,

I hope you're having a wonderful day swimming in Lake Baikal! I wanted to write to tell you how much I admire your dedication to seal conservation.

Today I picked up trash at the beach and thought of you. You're such an inspiration to all of us!

What's your favorite type of fish? I bet it's salmon!

Keep being awesome, Gennady!

Your biggest fan,
[Your Name]
